Authly Project Demo

A habit tracker with a weird authentication system

How It Works

  • Sign up with a username and a secret number
  • To login, solve a math puzzle using your secret number
  • Track daily habits and build streaks
  • Compete on the leaderboard

The Weird Auth Concept

Instead of passwords, you choose a secret number. When you login, the system generates a math puzzle using your number as "x". You solve the equation and enter the result. For example, if your secret number is 4, you might get:

x × 3 = ?
You solve: 4 × 3 = 12
You enter: 12

Or you might get:

(x × 2) + 5 = ?
You solve: (4 × 2) + 5 = 8 + 5 = 13
You enter: 13

This way, you never type your secret number directly. You solve for the result instead! The puzzle changes each time you login.
